Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions from parents

What are your hours?

We are open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 5:30 PM. We are closed on weekends and major holidays.

What ages do you accept?

We accept children from 6 weeks to 5 years of age. We have programs tailored for infants, toddlers, and preschoolers.

Do you provide meals?

Yes! We provide a nutritious morning snack, lunch, and afternoon snack. We can accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies.

What is your sick policy?

Children with fever, vomiting, or contagious illness must stay home. We follow state guidelines for illness exclusion to keep all children healthy.

Is there a waiting list?

Depending on current enrollment, there may be a waiting list for certain age groups. Contact us to learn about current availability.

How do I enroll?

Start by scheduling a tour! After your visit, we'll provide enrollment forms and discuss availability and start dates.
