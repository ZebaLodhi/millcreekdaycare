MILLCREEK HOME CHILDCARE - IMAGE PLACEHOLDERS
==============================================

Please add the following images to this folder:

1. hero-children.jpg
   - A happy image of children playing or learning
   - Recommended size: 500x400 pixels minimum
   - This is the main hero image on the homepage

2. /brand/ folder:
   - logo.png - Your logo (recommended: 200x80 pixels)
   - favicon.ico - Small icon for browser tabs (16x16 or 32x32)

3. /images/programs/ folder:
   - infant.jpg - Image representing infant care
   - toddler.jpg - Image representing toddler program
   - preschool.jpg - Image representing preschool

4. og-image.jpg
   - Social media preview image
   - Recommended size: 1200x630 pixels

TIPS:
- Use high-quality, cheerful photos of children in educational settings
- Ensure you have proper permissions/releases for any photos of children
- Optimize images for web (compress without losing quality)
- Use .jpg for photos, .png for logos with transparency
