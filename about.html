About Millcreek Home Childcare

Learn about our story, our team, and our commitment to nurturing young minds

Our Story

A Dream Built on Love for Children

Millcreek Home Childcare was founded with a simple yet powerful belief: every child deserves a nurturing environment where they can grow, learn, and discover their unique potential.

What started as a passion for early childhood education has blossomed into a beloved community childcare home. Our journey began over a decade ago when our founder, inspired by her own experience as a mother and educator, created a space where children could thrive in a home-like setting.

Today, we continue to honor that vision by providing exceptional care that balances structured learning with the freedom of play-based discovery.

Our Approach

Learning Through Play & Discovery

At Millcreek, we believe that children are natural learners. Our approach combines the warmth of home-based care with developmentally appropriate practices that foster curiosity and creativity.

We create an environment where children feel safe to explore, ask questions, and make discoveries. Our small group sizes ensure that each child receives individual attention and care tailored to their unique developmental needs.

Through hands-on activities, outdoor exploration, creative arts, and social play, we help children build the foundation for lifelong learning and success.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Safety First

Your child's safety is our top priority. Our home is fully childproofed, and we maintain strict supervision and safety protocols at all times.

Respect & Kindness

We treat every child with respect and teach them to be kind to others. Building positive relationships is at the heart of our program.

Individual Growth

Every child develops at their own pace. We celebrate individual milestones and provide support tailored to each child's unique journey.

Family Partnership

We believe in building strong partnerships with families. Open communication and collaboration help us provide the best care possible.

Joyful Learning

Learning should be fun! We create engaging experiences that spark curiosity and help children develop a love for discovery.

Nurturing Environment

Our cozy home setting provides comfort and security, helping children feel at ease and ready to explore the world around them.

Our Credentials

Licensed & Certified Care

Millcreek Home Childcare is fully licensed by the state and meets all regulatory requirements for home-based childcare. We maintain:

  • State childcare license in good standing
  • Current CPR and First Aid certifications for all caregivers
  • Regular health and safety inspections
  • Comprehensive liability insurance
  • Ongoing professional development and training
  • Background checks for all staff members
