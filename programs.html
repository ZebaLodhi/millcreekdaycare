Infant Care Program

6 Weeks - 18 Months

Our infant program provides a warm, nurturing environment where your baby will receive loving, responsive care. We understand the precious bond between parent and child, and we strive to create that same sense of security and trust.

Each infant follows their own schedule for feeding, sleeping, and play. Our experienced caregivers respond promptly to your baby's cues, building secure attachments that support healthy development.

Program Highlights:

  • Individualized feeding and sleeping schedules
  • Tummy time and motor skill development activities
  • Sensory exploration with age-appropriate toys and materials
  • Music, songs, and language-rich interactions
  • Daily communication with parents about your baby's day
  • Safe, clean environment with strict hygiene practices

Toddler Program

18 Months - 3 Years

Toddlers are busy explorers! Our toddler program channels their natural curiosity and energy into meaningful learning experiences. We create a safe environment where they can climb, run, build, and discover.

During this exciting stage of rapid development, we focus on language acquisition, social skills, independence, and motor development through hands-on, play-based activities.

Program Highlights:

  • Language-rich environment with songs, stories, and conversations
  • Art activities for creative expression and fine motor skills
  • Sensory play with sand, water, playdough, and more
  • Beginning social skills—sharing, taking turns, and making friends
  • Gross motor activities—climbing, dancing, and outdoor play
  • Introduction to self-help skills and potty training support

Preschool Program

3 - 5 Years

Our preschool program prepares children for the exciting transition to kindergarten while keeping learning fun and engaging. We use a balanced approach that combines structured learning with plenty of time for play and exploration.

Children develop early literacy and math skills, strengthen their social-emotional abilities, and build the confidence they need to succeed in their academic journey.

Program Highlights:

  • Pre-reading skills—letter recognition, phonics, and print awareness
  • Early math concepts—counting, sorting, patterns, and shapes
  • Science exploration and nature discovery activities
  • Creative arts—painting, drawing, crafts, and dramatic play
  • Social skills and emotional regulation development
  • Kindergarten readiness preparation

A Day at Millcreek

Our daily routine provides structure while allowing flexibility for individual needs

Morning Arrival

7:00 - 8:30 AM
Warm welcomes, free play, and settling in. Children choose activities as they arrive while caregivers greet families.

Circle Time & Learning

8:30 - 10:00 AM
Group activities including songs, stories, calendar, and age-appropriate learning experiences.

Snack & Outdoor Play

10:00 - 11:30 AM
Healthy morning snack followed by outdoor exploration, gross motor activities, and nature discovery.

Lunch & Rest Time

11:30 AM - 2:30 PM
Nutritious lunch, quiet activities, and nap or rest time for growing bodies and minds.

Afternoon Activities

2:30 - 4:30 PM
Art projects, sensory play, small group activities, and individual exploration time.

Evening Wind-Down

4:30 - 5:30 PM
Quiet play, snack time, and preparing for pickup. Daily updates shared with families.

Areas of Learning

Our curriculum covers all domains of early childhood development

Language & Literacy

Building vocabulary, reading readiness, and communication skills through stories, songs, and conversations.

Math & Logic

Exploring numbers, patterns, shapes, and problem-solving through hands-on activities and games.

Science & Discovery

Encouraging curiosity about the natural world through experiments, observation, and exploration.

Creative Arts

Self-expression through painting, drawing, music, dramatic play, and creative movement.

Social-Emotional

Developing friendships, emotional regulation, empathy, and positive social interactions.

Physical Development

Building both fine and gross motor skills through active play, art activities, and outdoor exploration.
